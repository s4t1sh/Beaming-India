Project Overview

The Snehit application was launched as part of the Special Malnutrition Eradication Campaign. Under this initiative, officials have been actively engaged in visiting the homes of malnourished children to gather essential information such as their name, father's name, date of birth, blood group, height, weight, address etc. Over the past three months, approximately 100 government officials have successfully identified and provided nutritional support to around 200 malnourished children, with 65 of them showing significant improvement.

The Need

Malnutrition has been a persistent challenge, necessitating a comprehensive strategy. The prevalence of malnutrition demanded a systematic strategy to address the root causes and implement targeted measures for the well-being of affected children.

Our Approach

The app, designed for Android platforms, serves as a powerful tool for collecting precise information on malnourished children, including their personal details, health metrics, and residential addresses. Snehit app facilitates efficient management by providing real-time, accurate information to the administration.
